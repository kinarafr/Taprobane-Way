Ella

4.9 (180+ reviews) | Central Province, Sri Lanka

Perched high in the lush, emerald hills of Sri Lanka's Central Highlands, Ella is a dreamy mountain sanctuary where cool highland breezes sweep across sprawling tea estates and dramatic cliff-sides. A magnet for adventurers and wellness travelers alike, Ella offers a peaceful refuge filled with spectacular trekking paths, historic railway monuments, and tumbling waterfalls.

Unlike the warm coastal plains below, Ella enjoys a refreshingly temperate climate. The town is surrounded by rolling green hills carpeted with Ceylon tea bushes. The slow-paced mountain culture makes it the perfect base to relax at cozy cafes, sample local Sri Lankan curries, and reconnect with nature. Mornings in Ella often start with thick blankets of mist clinging to the valleys, slowly clearing to reveal unrivaled panoramic vistas of the southern plains through the famous Ella Gap.

Whether you are taking in the grand colonial architecture of the Nine Arch Bridge, hiking up the steep, rewarding cliffs of Ella Rock, or watching the sunrise from the peak of Little Adam's Peak, Ella is a destination that inspires reflection and adventure. It embodies the majestic upland beauty of Sri Lanka.

Signature Experiences in Ella

Nine Arch Bridge

An architectural marvel of the British colonial era, this massive stone viaduct stands hidden in a lush forest. Walk along the tracks or sit at a nearby tea cafe to watch the iconic blue train pass over the bridge.

Little Adam's Peak

A gentle, picturesque hike that winds through fragrant tea plantations to a panoramic ridge. The path is accessible and offers stunning 360-degree views of the surrounding valleys and valleys below.

Ravana Falls

Located just a short drive from Ella town, this cascading waterfall gushes down a rugged rock face. According to legend, the cave behind the falls is where King Ravana hid the princess Sita.

Ceylon Tea Experience

Visit a functioning local tea factory to witness how the world-famous Ceylon tea leaves are plucked, dried, rolled, and graded. Conclude your tour with a private tasting session of rich infusions.

Traveler's Guide

Best Time to Visit

January to May brings dry days, sunny skies, and clear visibility across the high-altitude trails.

Getting There

Take the iconic hill country train from Kandy for a legendary, slow-speed scenic journey, or hire a vehicle for a 5.5-hour drive from Colombo.

Suggested Duration

2 to 3 days to hike the major peaks, capture the Nine Arch Bridge, and enjoy the relaxed local café culture.

Serene Tip

Start your hike to Little Adam's Peak around 5:15 AM. The cool morning air and the view of the sun rising above the misty mountain pass is unforgettable.
