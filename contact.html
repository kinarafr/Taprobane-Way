Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to help you plan your journey to Sri Lanka.

Do I need a visa?

Yes, most visitors need an Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) to enter Sri Lanka. It can be easily applied for online prior to arrival.

Learn more

When is the best time to visit?

Sri Lanka is a year-round destination. The west and south coasts are best from December to March, while the east coast is ideal from May to September.

How do we get around?

We typically arrange comfortable, air-conditioned private vehicles with experienced driver-guides for a seamless and safe travel experience.
